About Us

Excellence in Dental Care by Dr. Mohammed Farooq Mustafa

At Atmos Dental, we believe that a healthy smile transforms your confidence and quality of life. Under the expert care of Dr. Mohammed Farooq Mustafa, our clinic combines advanced dental technology with a warm, patient-first approach to deliver exceptional care.

Our Dental Services

Comprehensive dental care tailored to your needs, delivered with precision and compassion.

General Dentistry

Comprehensive check-ups, cleanings, fillings, and preventive care to keep your teeth strong and healthy year-round.

Teeth Whitening

Professional-grade whitening treatments that safely brighten your smile by several shades in just one visit.

Orthodontics / Braces

Metal braces, ceramic braces, and clear aligner solutions to straighten misaligned teeth at any age.

Dental Implants

Permanent, natural-looking tooth replacements that restore full chewing function and confidence in your smile.

Root Canal Treatment

Gentle, effective root canal procedures to save infected teeth and eliminate pain with minimal discomfort.

Smile Designing

Comprehensive aesthetic smile makeovers designed to enhance your natural beauty and boost your confidence.

Kids Dentistry

Child-friendly dental care in a fun, stress-free environment to build positive dental habits from an early age.

Aligners

Clear, comfortable aligners for discreet teeth straightening — achieve a perfect smile without visible braces.

Crowns & Bridges

Custom-crafted dental crowns and bridges that restore damaged or missing teeth with natural-looking durability.

Dentures

Comfortable, well-fitting partial and full dentures that restore your smile and confidence with a natural appearance.

Emergency Dental Care

Prompt same-day treatment for toothaches, broken teeth, lost fillings, and all urgent dental emergencies.
